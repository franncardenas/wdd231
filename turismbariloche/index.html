About Bariloche

Argentinian Flag

Bariloche Emblem

San Carlos de Bariloche, located in Argentina's RÃ­o Negro province, is one of Patagonia's most iconic travel destinations. Surrounded by lakes, mountains, and forests, it attracts visitors from all over the world.

Famous for its alpine-style architecture and artisanal chocolate, Bariloche offers year-round activities: skiing in winter, hiking and water sports in summer, plus a rich culinary scene. Close to Nahuel Huapi National Park, it's also a hub for science and education. Bariloche blends nature, adventure, and culture like no other place.
