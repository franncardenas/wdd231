Tourism in Bariloche - Site Plan

Site Name

Tourism in Bariloche

This name represents a travel and tourism site focused on exploring the natural beauty, culture, and activities available in San Carlos de Bariloche, Argentina. It is meant to attract travelers who are planning a trip to Patagonia.

Optional domain availability: tourisminbariloche.com

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a comprehensive travel guide for people planning to visit Bariloche. It will provide information about popular hiking trails, accommodations, local gastronomy, sightseeing spots, and outdoor activities. It will also include a contact form for inquiries and travel assistance.

Scenarios

  • What are the best hiking trails around Bariloche for beginners?
  • Where can I book a cabin or hotel near the Nahuel Huapi Lake?

Color Scheme

  • #0B3D91 (Dark Blue): Used for headers and navigation background
  • #F2F2F2 (Light Gray): Used for the main background and body text areas

Typography

  • Roboto: Used for body text (paragraphs and general content)
  • Montserrat: Used for headings (h1, h2, h3)
